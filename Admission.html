Admissions at Shreyarth University are open for students who want to pursue quality education and build a successful career.

Eligibility

  • Completed 12th Standard from a recognized board
  • Minimum required percentage as per program
  • Valid entrance exam score (if required)

Admission Process

  1. Fill the application form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Verification by admission office
  4. Admission confirmation

Required Documents

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • Passport Size Photos
  • Aadhar Card
  • School Leaving Certificate

Apply Now

Students can apply online or visit the campus admission office.
